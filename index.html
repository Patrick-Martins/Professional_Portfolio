Interactive Animation for Danish Museum of Science Project

After having learned the amazing manipulation powers on SVGs using Adobe Illustrator, Javascript and GSAP, I helped my group on creating an interactive website about the impact of the light bulb in our society, for our client, the Danish Museum of Science & Technology, we all designed SVGs for different elements of the room scene and the slides.
